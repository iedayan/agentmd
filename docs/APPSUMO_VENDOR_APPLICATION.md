## 1. Product Overview

### What is AgentMD?

AgentMD is a **CI/CD platform for AI agents** that makes the [AGENTS.md](https://agents.md) standard executable. Developers write workflows in a single markdown file; AgentMD parses, validates, scores, and runs them—with built-in governance, approval workflows, and audit trails.

### One-Line Pitch

> **Make your repository agent-ready. Define workflows in AGENTS.md. AgentMD executes them with governance built in.**

### Target Customer

- **Primary:** Solo developers, indie hackers, and small dev teams (1–10 people) building with AI agents
- **Secondary:** Startups and SMBs adopting agentic AI who need governance before scaling
- **Use case:** Teams using Claude, Cursor, Copilot, or custom agents who want deterministic, auditable execution
